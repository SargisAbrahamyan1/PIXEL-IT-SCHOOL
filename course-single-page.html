    Course Overview

    The UX/UI Design Fundamentals course is a comprehensive and hands-on learning experience that introduces students to the exciting world of User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) design. This course is designed for beginners and individuals looking to transition into the field of UX/UI design. No prior design experience is required.

    Throughout the course, students will gain a deep understanding of the principles, methodologies, and tools used in creating engaging and user-centered digital experiences. The curriculum is carefully crafted to cover a wide range of essential topics, from user research and wireframing to prototyping and usability testing.
